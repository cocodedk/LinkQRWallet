Android Link QR Wallet

Turn every link into a scannable memory.

Link QR Wallet is a personal vault for URLs that matter. Paste a link, capture a QR, fetch the page title, and keep your collection searchable, sortable, and offline-ready.

Instant QR capture
Automatic title fetch
Offline-first storage
Share-in support

What it does

Save a URL, preview its QR instantly, and open it later with a tap. Search by title or domain, sort your library, and share QR codes straight from the app.
